Profiles

Captain Lyra Solis

Role: The official leader of AstraNova.
Background: A former space mission commander whose task was to oversee the evacuation of humanity from Earth. Now she governs the city's political and military affairs.
Traits: Disciplined and honest, but deep down she fears the city might collapse.
Conflict: Must decide whether to maintain order with strict laws or grant the citizens more freedom.

Dr. Vex Calder

Role: Chief scientist of the Engineers’ Zone.
Background: A genius who created most of the city’s energy sources, but is accused of conducting secret experiments in the Shadow Zone.
Traits: Sharp-minded, unpredictable, and dependent on his data implant.
Conflict: His knowledge could either save the city or destroy it.

Zoro “The Shadow Merchant”

Role: Legendary trader and information broker of the Trade Plateau.
Background: Once a pirate on Earth who smuggled resources; in AstraNova he has become the ruler of the black market.
Traits: Charismatic and always smiling, yet ready to betray anyone for the right price.
Conflict: Knows the secret about a possible portal to a new planet, but refuses to reveal it without immense payment or influence.
